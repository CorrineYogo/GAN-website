Our Journey

How GAN Began

From identifying a problem to building a movement of 3,000+ young people shaping Kenya's climate future.

75% Under 35 years
80% ASAL Lands

In a country where climate change is real...

Over 75% of Kenya's population is under 35. Nearly 80% of the country is classified as ASAL (Arid and Semi-Arid Lands)The areas most vulnerable to climate shocks.

Yet those experiencing climate change daily were excluded from decisions shaping their future.

We saw: Ideas without access. Resilience without recognition. Policies written about youth, but rarely with them.

So we began with a simple belief:
Those most affected must have the power to be heard.

Greenspace Action & Network
was born as a movement

A growing community of 3,000+ young people across Kenya, unified by one mission:

Turn silence into influence.
Turn youth into leaders of change.

Our Philosophy

What Drives Everything We Do

Driven by Urgency

Climate change is disrupting lives today. There is no time to wait.

Driven by Equity

Those most affected deserve the strongest voice in the room.

Driven by Possibility

A single empowered young person can shift an entire community.

We don't train youth to attend meetings.
We prepare them to shape systems.
