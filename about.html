Who We Are

Greenspace Action and Network

The Greenspace Action and Network (GAN) is a non-governmental organization founded on October 25, 2021, with a focus on environmental sustainability. Officially registered in Kenya on November 11, 2022 .

The organization operates under the principles of transforming communities through youth leadership to address ecological challenges and promote sustainable practices.

GAN's work is grounded in collaboration and inclusivity, partnering with local governments, international bodies, and community groups to expand its impact.

Vision

Empowered communities with sustainable livelihoods driving inclusive transformation.

Mission

To drive sustainable transformation through integrated environmental conservation, skills enhancement, inclusive advocacy, and strengthening resilience systems.

Our Core Values

ACTS

Four principles that guide every program, partnership, and community interaction we undertake.

A
Accountability

We own our actions and report transparently to the communities and partners we serve.

C
Collaboration

No one solves climate change alone. We build partnerships that multiply our impact.

T
Transformation

We pursue structural, lasting change — not just outputs and activities.

S
Sustainability

Every program is designed for long-term ecological and social impact.
