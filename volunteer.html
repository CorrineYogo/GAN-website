Volunteer With GAN

Join a growing community of passionate youth taking real action on sustainability change. No matter your skills There's a place for you.

Why Volunteer?

Be a Climate Champion

GAN volunteers are at the heart of everything we do from community localized actions to policy workshops to digital campaigns. When you volunteer with us, you gain real experience, mentorship, and the chance to make a measurable impact.

Field & Community Programs

Support community restoration activities, tree planting drives, river cleanups, and climate awareness campaigns on the ground.

Research & Data

Assist with data collection, monitoring and evaluation, research documentation, and impact reporting.

Communications & Social Media

Help grow GAN's digital presence through content creation, storytelling, photography, and social media management.

Youth Training & Facilitation

Support our Climate Fellowship Program as a facilitator, mentor, or peer trainer for youth capacity building sessions.

Technology & Innovation

Contribute technical skills to climate technology pilots, digital tools, data visualization, and innovation challenges.

Fundraising & Partnerships

Support resource mobilization, proposal writing, donor engagement, and partnership development activities.
